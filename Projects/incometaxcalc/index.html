Current Tax Slabs

Old Regime (Without deductions):

  • ₹0 – ₹2.5L → 0%
  • ₹2.5L – ₹5L → 5%
  • ₹5L – ₹10L → 20%
  • Above ₹10L → 30%

New Regime (With ₹75K std. deduction):

  • ₹0 – ₹3L → 0%
  • ₹3L – ₹6L → 5%
  • ₹6L – ₹9L → 10%
  • ₹9L – ₹12L → 15%
  • ₹12L – ₹15L → 20%
  • Above ₹15L → 30%
